# Jin Shu biography of Li Hao (涼武昭王李暠) — research notes

**Date:** 2026-05-10  
**Source:** Fang Xuanling et al., *Jin Shu* (《晉書》) juan 87, liezhuan 57 — text and aligned translations via Wikisource / Chinese Text Project.

## Why treat this as a flagship primary source

- Compiled **648 CE** under **Tang Taizong** (唐太宗), with Fang Xuanling (房玄齡) among the lead compilers.
- **Emperor Taizong** and **Li Hao** both sit on the same imperial-descent narrative that later Tang ritual honored (Li Hao as posthumous **興聖皇帝** in pedigree). The standard history is therefore not a distant folk tale but a **court-sanctioned portrait** of a known forefather.
- Preserves **continuous prose** on Hexi politics, personal testament, and court culture — unusually rich for a single ancestor this deep in the tree.

## Ancestry chain (opening of biography)

| Figure | Name | Note per *Jin Shu* |
|--------|------|---------------------|
| Han exemplar | 李廣 Li Guang | Li Hao = **16th-generation descendant** (十六世孫) |
| Early Han | 仲翔 Zhongxiang | General; died fighting Qiang at 素昌 / 狄道一帶 |
| | 伯考 Bokao | Buried father east of Didao; family **世為西州右姓** |
| 高祖 | 雍 Yong | 東莞太守 (Jin) |
| 曾祖 | 柔 Rou | 北地太守 (Jin) |
| 祖 | 弇 Yan | 張軌下武衛將軍、安世亭侯 — ties line to **Former Liang** founder |
| 父 | 昶 Chang | Early reputation; died young; **posthumous birth** of Li Hao |

**Interpretation for Three Kingdoms / Wei–Jin questions:** Yong and Rou held **Jin** commandery governorships; the northwestern corridor was administratively continuous from **Cao Wei** into **Western Jin** and then regional regimes (張氏前涼). The biography does **not** enumerate every generation between Li Guang and Li Hao; intermediate names in private genealogies (e.g. **李楼** b.255 CE, **李发** in our consolidated chart) should be **correlated cautiously** to this documentary spine.

## Passages especially useful for family narrative

1. **Letter / admonitions to sons** (传记连续数段): integrity vs worldly gain; wine and speech; anger and joy; rewards/punishments; studying past success/failure; “面牆而立，不成人也.”
2. **Prophecy:** 郭黁 to 宋繇 about **國土之分** and **騧草馬生白額駒**; later 宋繇 reminds 李暠 **白額駒今已生矣**.
3. **Deathbed:** strength failing at **67**; regret that **志不申**; charge to 宋繇 re young heir.
4. **Culture:** 靖恭堂, schools, 述志賦 / 槐樹賦 / 大酒容賦, 諸葛亮訓誡.

## Relationship detail (correction)

- **宋繇** is **郭黁’s maternal younger brother** (同母弟), not Li Hao’s sibling — but he is the figure who repeats the foal omen when Li Hao hesitates to take power.
